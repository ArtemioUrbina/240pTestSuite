              GRID

The grid uses a pattern that
fills all of the 4:3 visible
area. 

You can use it to verify that
all the whole area is being
displayed, and that there is no
distortion present. Consumer TVs
may not show the red squares.

Different grids are shown for
every video mode supported.

You should target the #Ggreen#G
borders on CRTs.

   White    90%      #GGreen#G 91%
   #MMagenta#M  95%      #CCyan#C  100%
